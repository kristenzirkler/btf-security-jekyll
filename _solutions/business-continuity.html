BTF Security Consulting, Inc. has a strong team that focuses on Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery (BCDR) plan following our protocol, procedures, and resources that assist businesses recover from a disaster. We strive to ensure that all critical business functions and technology systems are operating during and after a devastating disruption. We conduct assessments of the potential costs of the disruption, including loss of income, fines, and increased expenses. Our team focuses on the overall strategy to ensure that critical business functions continue during and after a disaster. These services includes risk management and planning to prevent interruptions to business operations. Our business continuity plans are company-wide and strategic, addressing all aspects of disaster planning. We concentrate on the technology infrastructure, with a strong emphasis on restoring data and applications after a disaster. Our team of experts aims to minimize downtime and data loss by having backup systems and processes in place. Our Disaster Recovery plans are more IT-focused and aim to get mission-critical applications up and running as quickly as possible.
