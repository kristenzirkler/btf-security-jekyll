This is a preparedness section for BTF Security Consulting Inc. to collect a list of highly qualified Subcontractors who will be called when specific needs arise that require experienced personnel in different regions of all 50 states and Puerto Rico that are able to respond to varying Crowd/Event Control. Search & Rescue and Natural Disaster scenarios such as hurricanes, tornadoes, flooding, uncontrolled fires, etcetera. If you are interested in being on our call list, please provide your name, phone number, address, email address, region of the US/PR, any particular license you hold, license expiration date, and any equipment you may have such as tractor w/ trailer, portable defibrillator, EMS/Paramedic equipment, flood lights, portable generators, temporary fencing, skid steer equipment, drone/drone operator license, ATV's, side-by-sides, etc. that may be used in rapid response scenarios. BTF Security Consulting Inc. will keep your information in our secure database and call all available personnel needed to respond in an emergency situation. Types of subcontractors include Private Investigators, EMT/Paramedics, Volunteer Fire Fighters, Heavy Equipment Operators, Small Engine Repairmen, On-Call Crisis Counselors, etc. BTF Security Consulting Inc. offers very competitive wages to all Subcontractors (pay is reflective of your particular area of expertise.)

*Disclaimer: All information collected for Subcontractors will be used for the sole purpose of creating a database/contact list for BTF Security Consulting Inc. and will not be shared with any outside agency and your information will not be sold to third party members. Database will be updated annually, and a courtesy reminder will be sent to licensed Subcontractors of license expiration date.
